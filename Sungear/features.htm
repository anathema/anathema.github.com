Character Management

It's as easy as never before. Featuring most rules and nearly every charm published, Anathema allows you to create and maintain characters on the fly. Use the PDF-Export feature and you're set to play.

  • 1st Edition, 1st Edition Power Combat and 2nd Edition Support
  • Supports all of the "core" exalts as well as (Heroic) Mortals
  • Fully graphical Charm selection
  • Optimizing calculator for Bonus Point spendings
  • Random Name Generator
  • "Overview" sidebar, providing all data at a glance

Charm Cascades

Building on the novel Charm view you already witnessed in Character Creation, the Charm Cascades allow you to browse the database and examine the complete game statistics for all Charms published by White Wolf.

  • One gazillion Charm entries
  • Easily compare different editions of a Charm

PDF Export

When your group gathers at the table, your data comes with you: Just export everything you created to PDF files and have them printed wherever the Adobe Reader is available.

  • Print characters to an unique sheet inspired by voidstate
  • Print your series to a novel-like format, complete with table of contents
