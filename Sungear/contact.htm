Direct Contact

If you want to contact the team directly, simply . This is mostly used if you are interested in contributing to the project, please do not use e-mail to report bugs or to request features.

You can also visit the SourceForge Anathema Project Forum and post there. The system is frequently checked by numerous team members. It also allows anonymous posting so you are not required to register.

External Communication

Urs is keeping a livejournal called "The Wyld Hunt" which documents the latest developments on Anathema. If you like some insight into or whining about the development proccess, go there. Sometimes he also posts exclusive screenshots. You can also leave comments there.

The project is also discussed in certain threads within a number of external forums. These forums and posts are frequented by the team.
