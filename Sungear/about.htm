What is Anathema?

Anathema is A Nascent Approach To Harmonic Exalted MAnagement.

Anathema is a freely available all-round Exalted toolkit, offering a multitude of helpful functions that make life easier for both storytellers and players. This includes:

  • Character creation and experience management
  • Character Sheet PDF export and printing support
  • Extensive visual charm cascades
  • Series management
  • A music database

For more information and screenshots, please see the Features page.

Anathema Development

Anathema is free software developed by fans and enthusiasts of the Exalted game. Everyone working on the project does so in their spare time and without compensation. If you want to support the program and its developers, please consider donating.
