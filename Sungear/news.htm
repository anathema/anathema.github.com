New website unveiled

posted by Urs on 05/09/2006

After three month of discussion, labor, more discussion and equal amounts of refinement, we are proud to present the new Anathema website. Crafted by Martin Nerukar, known to the net as Democritus, it marks the first step towards a new, more mature look of the entire project.

If you asked Martin where to go from here, he'd recommend the features page, there to marvel at the brand new screenshots. I, on the other hand, would point you to development. Go there if you want to know what else is hot.

No matter your decision, we hope you enjoy your stay and, as always, Anathema.
